I am a senior researcher at NVIDIA Research working on graphics programming languages. I received my PhD degree on Computer Sceience from Carnegie Mellon University in September 2018. I have benefited in many ways from my advisor, Kayvon Fatahalian. My PhD study was focused on optimizing graphics systems with language mechanisms and compilation techniques.

Before that, I received my bachelor degree in software engineering from South China University of Technology, P.R. China, in 2011.
